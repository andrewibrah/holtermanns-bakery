Our Story

A Staten Island bakery since 1878.

Holtermann's Bakery is a family-owned Staten Island bakery with roots reaching back to 1878. For generations, local families have stopped in for fresh bread, donuts, cookies, cakes, pies, pastries, and celebration desserts made with the kind of old-school bakery character that is getting harder to find.

Part of Staten Island memory

More than a bakery.

Customers talk about the smell when they walk in, the counter service, the crumb cake and coffee rings, the jelly donuts, the fresh breads, and the hard-to-find classics like Charlotte Russe. It's the kind of place where grandparents, parents, and children can all have a favorite.

As one third-generation customer put it: "The crumb cakes and coffee rings are the best. They still make delicious kringle."

Old-school craft

Fresh-baked, the way it's always been.

The bakery case changes with the day and the season, but the heart stays the same: fresh-baked goods, familiar recipes, and a wide range of sweets and staples for everyday mornings, holidays, and family celebrations.

We also make cakes for birthdays, weddings, and special occasions. Whether you need a simple cake for the table or a decorated cake for a milestone, call or visit in person to talk through your order.
