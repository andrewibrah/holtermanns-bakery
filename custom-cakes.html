What we make

Cakes for every occasion.

From a simple cake for the table to a decorated centerpiece for a milestone, we'll walk you through flavor, filling, icing, size, and design.

Birthday Cakes

Classic layer cakes and cupcakes made to order — praised by customers as "made exactly as instructed and ready on time."

Wedding Cakes

How to order

Ordering is simple — call or stop in.

We take custom orders by phone or in person so we can get every detail right for your celebration.

1

Call or visit

Reach us at (718) 984-7095 or come to the counter at 405 Arthur Kill Rd. Have your date and headcount ready.

2

Plan the details

We'll help you choose cake flavor, filling, icing, size, writing, and decoration to fit your occasion and budget.

3

Pick it up fresh

We'll confirm your pickup date and time so your cake is decorated, boxed, and ready when you need it.

📞 Call to Start Your Order

For wedding cakes and large orders, please call ahead — minimum notice and details vary by season, so we'll confirm timing with you.
