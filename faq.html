Good to know

Frequently asked questions.

Quick answers about hours, orders, cakes, and our classics. When in doubt, give us a call — we're happy to help.

Where is Holtermann's Bakery located?

Holtermann's Bakery is located at 405 Arthur Kill Rd, Staten Island, NY 10308, near Historic Richmondtown. Get directions →

What is the phone number?

Call us at (718) 984-7095.

What are your hours?

Monday 7:30 AM–4:30 PM, Tuesday through Saturday 7:30 AM–5:30 PM, and Sunday 7:30 AM–4:30 PM. Holiday hours may vary — please confirm by phone.

Do you take orders online?

Orders are handled by phone or in person. Call (718) 984-7095 or stop by the counter to place an order.

Do you make birthday cakes?

Yes — birthday cakes and custom celebration cakes are one of our specialties. Call or stop in to discuss size, design, flavor, and pickup timing. See our Custom Cakes page.

Do you make wedding cakes?

Yes, we make wedding cakes and specialty cakes. Because details and timing vary, please call us directly to plan your cake.

What is Holtermann's known for?

Customers most often mention our donuts, jelly donuts, old-fashioned donuts, Charlotte Russe, crumb cake, coffee rings, pecan rings, banana cake, eclairs, fresh breads, cookies, pies, pastries, and custom cakes.

Do you sell Charlotte Russe?

Charlotte Russe is one of our beloved, hard-to-find classics. Availability can vary by day and season, so please call ahead to confirm.

Do you offer gluten-free or sugar-free options?

Availability of gluten-free or sugar-free items varies. Please call us before ordering so we can confirm what's available and answer any questions about ingredients.

Do you ship?

Please call to ask about pickup and any current options — we'll let you know what we can do.

Is merchandise available?

Yes — you can support the bakery through our merch store. Visit the merch store →

Are prices listed online?

Because our case changes daily and seasonally, we keep pricing at the counter. Please call for current availability and pricing: (718) 984-7095.
